The Perfect Ellipse, Dosa

Ingredients Required:

  • Dosa batter
    • That's it yes.

Cooking Instructions:

  1. Take a ladle full of the batter. Pour the dosa batter and gently spread the batter starting from the center and moving outwards.
  2. Cover it with a lid and cook the dosa on a low to medium heat. Do regulate the flame as per the pan size and thickness.
  3. When you see the batter on the top has cooked well and the bottom has become crisp and golden, then sprinkle ¼ to ½ teaspoon oil on the edges and center.
  4. With the spoon spread the oil on the dosa.
  5. Cook till the base is nicely golden and crisp. The base will leave the pan and the sides will also get separated when it gets cooked.
  6. Fold and serve sada dosa hot. Make all dosai this way.
  7. Serve these crisp plain dosa with sambar or potato masala or coconut chutney
